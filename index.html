Gourmet Menu

You’ll find everything from soups and salads to complete meals, breads, cakes and pastries and more. A typical meal might contain beef tenderloin with a demiglace sauce, served with scalloped potatoes and steamed green beans.

La Dolce Vita Preserves

These preserves are locally sourced wherever possible, prepared to traditional recipes using no artificial colouring or preservatives, made in the traditional manner, and produced in small batches to ensure quality.

Dirty Duck Soaps

Fat from practical cooking classes is now collected, cleanly processed (with heat and water), and used as the base for our culinary soaps. All the soaps are created with as few ingredients as possible, maintaining our “waste not, want not” mandate.

The Chefs

Savoir Fare is the gourmet food store run by the School of Hospitality and Tourism’s culinary department. Algonquin’ culinary and baking students prepare all the food, including our exclusive line of La Dolce Vita preserves and Dirty Duck Soaps.
